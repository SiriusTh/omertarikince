The second session of Haydarpaşa MUN was held between 28th of January and 1st of February 2018 in Haydarpaşa High School. The executive team consisted of; Secretary General Burak Çiçek, Deputy Secretary General Berra Taşdemir, Director General Mert Şahin and Deputy Director Generals Burak Macit and Merve İçin. Total number of 300 delegates attended 9 committees which were:

  • SOCHUM
  • DISEC
  • Organisation of Islamic Cooperation
  • Lincoln's Cabinet: American Civil War
  • JCC: Axis vs Allies
  • Game of Thrones
  • UNSC
  • INTERPOL
  • Futuristic Military Committee of NATO
